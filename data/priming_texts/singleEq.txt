# On Saturday, Sara spent $10.62 each on 2 tickets to a movie theater. Sara also rented a movie for $1.59, and bought a movie for $13.95. Write a program that prints How much money in total did Sara spend on movies?

nr_tickets = 2
movie_rent = 1.59
movie_buy = 13.95
print(ticket_price * nr_tickets + movie_rent + movie_buy)

# Bryan took a look at his books as well. If Bryan has 56 books in each of his 9 bookshelves, write a program that prints How many books does he have in total?

print(total_books)
