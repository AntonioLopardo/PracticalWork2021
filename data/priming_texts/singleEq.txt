#Joan found 70 seashells on the beach. she gave Sam some of her seashells. She has 27 seashell left. How many seashells did she give to Sam ?
joan_shells_before = 70
joan_shells_after = 27
sam_shells = Joan_shells_before - Joan_shells_after
print(Sam_shells)

#Zach wants to ride the Ferris wheel, the roller coaster, and the log ride. The Ferris wheel costs 2 tickets, the roller coaster costs 7 tickets and the log ride costs 1 ticket. Zach has 1 ticket. How many more tickets should Zach buy?
cost_ferris_wheel = 2
cost_roller_coaster = 7
cost_log_ride = 1
zach_tickets = 1
total_cost = cost_ferris_wheel + cost_log_ride + cost_roller_coaster
additional_tickets_needed = total_cost - zach_tickets
print(additional_tickets_needed)
